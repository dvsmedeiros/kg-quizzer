To make a basic cake using 300 grams of flour, follow this simple recipe:

**Ingredients**:
- 300g all-purpose flour  
- 200g granulated sugar  
- 150g unsalted butter (softened)  
- 150ml milk  
- 2 large eggs  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- Pinch of salt  

**Instructions**:
1. **Preheat** the oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and flour a round or square cake pan.  
2. **Cream** the butter and sugar in a bowl until light and fluffy.  
3. **Beat in** the eggs one at a time, then mix in the vanilla extract.  
4. **Sift** the flour, baking powder, and salt into another bowl.  
5. **Alternate** adding the dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, mixing gently until just combined.  
6. **Pour** the batter into the prepared pan.  
7. **Bake** for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.  
8. **Cool** the cake in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.  

**Tips**:  
- For a richer flavor, use buttermilk instead of regular milk.  
- Add 100g chocolate chips or fruit (e.g., berries) for variations.  
- Avoid overmixing to prevent a dense texture.  

Enjoy your cake! 🍰
